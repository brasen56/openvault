How Tier 1 (Keyword Filter) works

Tier 1 is a fast, free keyword heuristic. It:

  • Only catches contradictions when both memories are co-retrieved in the same turn (it runs after scoring & budgeting).
  • Does not backfill freed budget when a memory is suppressed.
  • Suppresses the entire losing sentiment side for a character group until a newer memory of the opposite sentiment appears.

For nuanced contradictions, enable LLM Contradiction Analysis (Tier 2) above.
